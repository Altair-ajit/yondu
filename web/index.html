ENEE 408J · Team Yondu · UMD Capstone 2025

Whistle. Fly.

Our capstone was a control system that turns audio frequencies into machine commands — a mic on an ESP32, FFT on a Raspberry Pi, instructions out a serial port (we demoed it on a rover). This is its continuous-control mode, reborn in your browser: calibrate a personal whistle print, then steer a yaka arrow with nothing but pitch.

Audio never leaves your device — all processing is local. Chrome/Edge/Firefox recommended.
